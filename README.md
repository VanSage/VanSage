# 💎 About Me

I am a **Final Year Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) student** with a strong passion for **Data Analytics, Business Intelligence, and Data Visualization**.

I enjoy transforming messy, real-world datasets into meaningful dashboards, reports, and insights that support better decision-making. My projects focus on solving practical business problems using **Python, SQL, Excel, and Power BI**, with an emphasis on clear storytelling and measurable outcomes.
